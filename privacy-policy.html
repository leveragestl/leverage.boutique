1. Overview

Leverage ("we," "us," or "our") operates three Shopify applications: Product Badges, Low Stock Alerts, and COGS Margin Tracker (collectively, the "Apps"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you install and use our Apps, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

Our Apps are installed by Shopify merchants ("you" or "merchant") and operate within the Shopify ecosystem. By installing any of our Apps, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Short version: We collect only what we need to operate the apps. We don't sell your data. We don't use it for advertising. We delete it when you uninstall.

2. Data we collect

The data we collect varies by app. The table below outlines what each app accesses and stores:

Data type Product Badges Low Stock Alerts COGS Tracker
Shop domain & access token Yes Yes Yes
Product IDs, titles, variants Yes (for badge assignment) Yes (for alert context) Yes (for margin tracking)
Inventory levels Yes (auto low-stock rule only) Yes (core functionality) No

Shopify OAuth access tokens are stored securely in our database with encryption at rest provided by our hosting infrastructure. We do not log access tokens in plaintext under any circumstances.

None of our Apps collect customer personal data. COGS Tracker accesses order data for margin calculations, but stores only product titles, quantities, and financial amounts — not customer names, email addresses, physical addresses, or payment information.

3. How we use your data

We use the data collected solely to operate and improve our Apps. Specifically:

  • Product Badges: Product data is used to assign badges and write badge configuration metafields to your products. Inventory data is used only if you enable the "Low Stock" auto-rule badge type.
  • Low Stock Alerts: Inventory levels are monitored in real-time via webhooks to determine when an alert should be sent. Merchant email addresses are used only to deliver alerts you've configured.
  • COGS Margin Tracker: Product cost data, order line items, and merchant-entered expenses are used to calculate and display profit margin metrics. Aggregated, anonymous metrics may be sent to Anthropic's Claude API for AI-powered analysis when the merchant requests it. Data is never used outside the app context.

We do not use your data for:

  • Advertising or marketing to third parties
  • Profiling or behavioral analysis beyond the app's stated purpose
  • Training machine learning models
  • Any purpose unrelated to operating the Apps

4. Data sharing and disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data with any third party. We share data only with the following subprocessors, solely as required to operate the Apps:

  • Supabase (database hosting): Our PostgreSQL database hosted on Supabase's infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified. Supabase Privacy Policy →
  • Railway (application hosting): Our server infrastructure. Railway does not access your data. Railway Privacy Policy →
  • Resend (email delivery, Low Stock Alerts only): Merchant email addresses are passed to Resend only to deliver alerts you've configured. Resend does not retain email content beyond delivery. Resend Privacy Policy →
  • Anthropic (AI analysis, Low Stock Alerts and COGS Tracker): Aggregated store metrics (revenue totals, margin percentages, top product names and performance) are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate insights and recommendations. No customer data or personally identifiable information is included. Anthropic Privacy Policy →

We may disclose your data if required by law, regulation, or court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Leverage, our users, or the public.

5. Data retention

We retain your data only for as long as your Apps are installed. When you uninstall any of our Apps, all associated store data is deleted from our systems within 48 hours of receiving the app/uninstalled webhook from Shopify.

This includes:

  • OAuth session records and access tokens
  • Badge configurations and assignment data (Product Badges)
  • Alert configurations, thresholds, and alert log history (Low Stock Alerts)
  • Product cost data, order line items, and margin snapshot data (COGS Tracker)

Alert history logs (Low Stock Alerts) are retained for a maximum of 30 days on a rolling basis while the app is installed.

COGS Tracker retains historical order line item data for the lifetime of your subscription. On uninstall, this data is deleted in full.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS. HTTP connections are rejected.
  • Shopify OAuth access tokens are stored securely with encryption at rest provided by our database infrastructure (Supabase).
  • All Shopify webhook payloads are validated using HMAC-SHA256 signature verification before processing.
  • Each app uses a separate, isolated database so no data is shared between apps.
  • We do not log access tokens, customer payment data, or other sensitive fields.

No security system is perfect. If you discover a security vulnerability in any of our Apps, please report it to security@leverageapps.com.

7. GDPR rights (EEA merchants)

If you are a merchant in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data. Uninstalling the app triggers automatic deletion within 48 hours.
  • Right to restriction: You may request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: You may request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object: You may object to certain types of processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@leverageapps.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Our legal basis for processing your data is the performance of a contract — specifically, to provide the App services you have subscribed to.

GDPR Data Request Handling

In compliance with Shopify's GDPR requirements, we implement all mandatory data request webhooks: customer data requests, customer data redaction, and shop data redaction.

Because our Apps do not store customer personal data, customer data requests and redaction requests are automatically acknowledged. Shop data redaction triggers full deletion of all store data from our systems.

8. CCPA rights (California merchants)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to know: You have the right to request that we disclose what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell.
  • Right to delete: You have the right to request that we delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to opt-out: We do not sell personal information. There is nothing to opt out of.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To submit a CCPA request, contact us at privacy@leverageapps.com.

9. A note on your customers

Our Apps are installed by Shopify merchants. If you are a merchant using our Apps, you are responsible for ensuring that your use of our Apps complies with applicable privacy laws as they relate to your own customers.

None of our Apps collect, store, or process any personal data about your end customers. They operate entirely at the store-admin, inventory, and financial level.

COGS Margin Tracker accesses order data to calculate margins, but stores only order numbers, product titles, quantities, and financial amounts. No customer names, email addresses, physical addresses, or payment information is stored.
