Common questions.

Do I need a specific Shopify plan?

No. Low Stock Alerts works on every Shopify plan including Basic ($29/month). Shopify Flow — the alternative — requires the $79/month Shopify plan or higher. That's a big difference if you're a small store.

How quickly does the alert arrive?

Typically within 60 seconds. We use Shopify's real-time inventory webhooks, which fire the moment inventory changes. We process it immediately and send the email right away.

What if inventory bounces around a threshold repeatedly?

The 24-hour cooldown handles this. If a product drops below threshold, we alert you. If it comes back up and drops again within 24 hours, we don't re-alert. This prevents inbox flooding during busy sales periods.

Can I set different thresholds for different products?

Yes. You set a global threshold (e.g., 5 units) and can override it per variant. So your hero product alerts at 20 units while a lower-priority accessory alerts at 2.

Will the app monitor products across multiple locations?

The app monitors total available inventory across all your locations. If the combined stock drops below your threshold, you get alerted. Per-location threshold monitoring may be added in a future update based on demand.
